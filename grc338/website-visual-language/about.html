Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, Butter & Bliss Bakery is a beloved local gem that delights residents and visitors alike with its diverse array of freshly baked goods. From buttery croissants that melt in your mouth to exquisitely decorated cupcakes that tempt even the most discerning palate, there's something to satisfy every craving at this charming bakery.

With a focus on quality and innovation, Butter & Bliss Bakery takes pride in offering an extensive menu of treats that cater to every taste and dietary preference. Whether you're in the mood for a classic French baguette or a gluten-free cake that rivals its traditional counterpart in flavor and texture, you'll find it here, lovingly crafted with the finest ingredients and attention to detail.

Butter & Bliss Bakery isn't just a place to pick up your favorite baked goods; it's a gathering spot where friends and family come together to share moments of joy over a cup of freshly brewed coffee and a pastry. The cozy atmosphere and friendly service make it the perfect destination for a leisurely morning brunch or an afternoon pick-me-up.

As a proud member of the San Luis Obispo community, Butter & Bliss Bakery is committed to supporting local farmers and businesses, sourcing ingredients locally whenever possible to ensure freshness and sustainability. With its dedication to excellence and passion for baking, this beloved bakery has become a cherished part of the culinary landscape in San Luis Obispo, where every bite is a taste of pure bliss.
